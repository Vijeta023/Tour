Detailed Itinerary:



Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur


We offer a private day tour in Jaipur City with a car, driver and guide. We suggest you try this tour of all the must-see places. This tour covers – Amber Fort with Elephant ride, Nahargarh Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar, Albert Hall, Birla Temple, Local Market



Tour Schedule:



08:00 – Our driver and guide will meet you at the hotel and proceed for the day tour of Jaipur,

09:00 – Visit Amber Fort with an Elephant ride. The fort is situated on a hill 12Kms from the city,

10:30 – Visit Nahargarh Fort - The smallest of the three forts, notable primarily for excellent views over Man Sagar lake and the vast sprawl of Jaipur.-

12:00 – Jantar Mantar - The Jantar Mantar is the largest of five astronomical observatory built by Maharaja Jai Singh during the period 1727-1734 in north India. It is located very close to the City Palace.

13:00 – Hawa Mahal

14:00 – Lunch at Indiana Cuisine

15:00 – Visit Albert Hall Museum,

17:00 – Local Market of Jaipur

19:30 – Drop at the hotel.
In between sightseeing, your guide will take you to a local restaurant to enjoy an authentic Indian lunch.



Tour Includes:



(1) Car
(2) Driver

(3) Lunch

(4) Arrival and departure, Sightseeing with air-conditioner car

(5) English-speaking guide or language guide

(6) Two bottles of mineral water

(7) All applicable taxes
