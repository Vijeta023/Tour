overview



A trip to Rajasthan is in order, especially if you’re an adventurer at heart. Get ready to explore five of the most famous cities and beautiful landscapes. Experience Udaipur and Jaipur, two of India’s best-known cities, before visiting Jodhpur, which was once a part of the British empire.

See sacred temples, enjoy village life with local families in tiny hamlets, hike through ancient forests and climb up mountains that were once sacred to Rao Bika himself.

Explore the majestic beauty of the desert as you take an 11 Days/10 Nights tour to discover this amazing state of Rajasthan.

On this itinerary, you will visit a few historical places like Jodhpur Fort in Jaipur, Rawla Chatar in Jodhpur and Ode Gate, Aawas and Amber Fort in Jaipur among other major attractions.

The trip starts with its setting in the magnificent fort of Jodhpur. After spending a day here you will be taken to the next stop Rawla Jojawar where you will be treated at 5 star hotel. we'll visit Udaipur and then to Bundi before visiting the temple of Ranthambhore which is famous for tigers.






Itinerary



Day 1: Arrived at Jodhpur

Welcome to India, You will be received by Incredible Tour To India representative at airport, we will be waiting for you, by holding you play card in his hand. He will assist you to check in your hotel.

In the Hotel you will have a brief description of your tour and the representative will hand over your documents regarding the tour, Overnight in Hotel. .


Day 2: Jodhpur

After breakfast proceed for city sightseeing tour of Jodhpur. Jodhpur – the stronghold of the fierce Rathore clan, was founded in 1459 by Prince Jodha. Visit the Mehrangarh Fort, situated on a low sandstone hill. Within the fort, visit Moti Mahal and Phool Mahal.

Also visit Jaswant Thada, an imposing marble cenotaph, built i n memory of Maharaja Jaswant singh II around 1899 and Umaid Public gardens. Over Night stay at hotel in Jodhpur.


Day 3: Rawla Jojawar

After breakfast driver to Rawla Jojawar, en route visit Ranakpur jain temple. Continue to Rawla jojawar, on arrival check in hotel and take leisure rest. After rest go to jeep safari. Overnight at hotel.


Day 4: Rawla Jojawar

Full day at Rawla Jojawar, Early morning train safari and afternoon horse riding, evening free for more activities. Overnight at Rawla Jojawewar. /p>


Day 5: Udaipur

After breakfast Drive to Udaipur (en route visit Kumbhal garh fort ) . On arrival check in hotel. Late afternoon enjoy a two hour boat-ride on Lake Pichola and enjoy excellent views of the Lake Palace and City Palace. Overnight at hotel.


Day 6: Udaipur

After breakfast explore beautiful city by your own visiting, The City Palace and Museum is considered as the largest palace complex in Rajasthan. Pratap Memorial has the bronze statue of Maharana Pratap, the legendary warrior king of Udaipur.

Jagdish Temple which was built in 1651 AD. Saheliyon Ki Bari is a small ornamental garden. Overnight at Hotel in Udaipur.


Day 7: Bundi

After breakfast drive to Bundi, en route visit Chittorgarh fort, continue to Bundi. On arrival check in hotel and take rest. After rest visit Bundi fort. Overnight at hotel.


Day 8: Ranthambhore

After breakfast drive to Ranthambhore, on arrival check in hotel and take rest. Overnight at hotel.


Day 9: Ranthambhore

After Early morning breakfast do a Jungle safari in a Gipsy (a vehicle which is open on the top) enjoy the game viewing in the national park and back to the hotel. After lunch second visit to jungle and enjoy the game viewing again. Overnight stay at hotel in Ranthambhore.


Day 10: Jaipur

After breakfast drive to Jaipur and upon arrival, check into your hotel. Evening visit Chokhi Dhani for traditional Rajasthani dinner (Optional). Overnight stay at hotel.


Day 11: Jaipur

Morning tour of the impressive Amber Fort, situated on a ridge just outside Jaipur City. At the foot of the hill you will mount your caparisoned elephant ride for the slow but steady climb up to the Main gate, making your entrance in the time honoured fashion.

The Fort, completed in the early 18th century, took over 100 years to build and now, although deserted, offers a fascinating insight into the lifestyle of the Moghul ruling families. Afternoon city tour of Jaipur – \\\”The Pink City\\\” is spectacularly set within surrounding rugged hills, dotted with ancient ruined fortresses. Your tour will include the imposing City Palace, still home to the Maharaja although several of the rooms are now open to the public.

Many antiques and artifacts belonging to the ruling family are housed here, including a collection of weaponry. You will also visit the Hawa Mahal \\\”Palace of the Winds\\\”, and the open – air Jantar Mantar Observatory to see the vast astronomical instruments. Enjoy a stroll through the colourful bazaar before returning to your hotel. Overnight at hotel.
