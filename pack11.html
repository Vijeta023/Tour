overview



Welcome to the state of Rajasthan where you will have the opportunity of viewing some of India’s renowned monuments. You will spend 13 days roaming this wonderful country and meet some nice people along the way. The itinerary includes Delhi and Agra (where Taj Mahal is located), Fatehpur Sikri, Jaipur and Pushkar.

The tour of Rajasthan to Himalaya tour will take you to the most beautiful places in and around Rajasthan, including Fatehpur Sikri and Jaipur.

The itinerary is fully suited for all types of travellers with breathtaking mountains, serene lakes and one of the best roads in India.Imagine yourself standing at the watchtower of Rajasthan on a hill top overlooking all that comes your way!

This tour provides you an exclusive opportunity to explore Rajasthan by visiting its famous forts, forts and palaces up close.

The Rajasthan with Himalaya Tour is a unique journey of 13 days, 12 nights. Our tour is perfect for any traveller looking for genuine experiences that let you explore the best that Rajasthan has to offer. It is an ideal choice for people who want to experience India like it really is – authentically and authentically.

The tour starts with a daylong city tour of Delhi in the morning, followed by two nights at the modern and serene Deluxe No. 1 Heritage Hotel in New Delhi. A drive to Agra in the morning and a night halt at Fatehpur Sikri with magnificent Taj Mahal is the next day's event.

After breakfast, the driver takes you to Jaipur where you stay at an upscale range of hotels at doorstep to the popular Jaipur Palace - first built for Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh II in 17th century.

You then proceed towards Pushkar before visiting Udaipur and en route you visit a village typical village of Rajasthan on cow dung road called Phalamgham, which is also known as 'Dungarji's Metropolis' as every household had dung-cycles. Finally you arrive at Srinagar after night halt to relax in one of its beautiful hill stations Sonmarg or Gulmarg.






Itinerary



Day 01: Arrive New Delhi

Welcome to India by Incredible Tour to India for Rajasthan with Himalaya Tour. on arrival assist by the driver and direct to the hotel.


Day 02: New Delhi and Old Delhi

After Breakfast sightseeing tour to Old Delhi visit the Raj Ghat - the cremation sites of Mahatma Gandhi , Jama Masjid and drive past Red Fort and Chandni Chowk.Qutub Minar built by Qutub-ud-Din Aibek in 1199, Humayuns Tomb, India Gate and Lakshminarayan Temple. Also drive past Presidents House, Parliament House. Overnight stay.


Day 03:- New Delhi to Agra

After early breakfast drives to Agra and check-in to hotel. After leisure rest visit the Agra Fort, Baby Taj and evening visit the Tajmahal with sunset. . Overnight stay Hotel.


Day 04:- Agra to Jaipur

Early morning visit sunrise TAJMAHAL and then go to hotel for breakfast,. After breakfast at hotel, drive to Jaipur en-route visit Fatehpur Sikri. After visit continue drive to Jaipur and check into hotel. Overnight at Hotel.( most invite for dinner at my home).


Day 05:- Jaipur

After breakfast enjoy visit of Amber Fort the old capital of rulers of Jaipur. Here one takes an elephant to ride to the palace situated on the hill. Afternoon visit city including City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal, Birla Mandir and Jai Singhs Observatory. Overnight at Hotel.


Day 06 Jaipur to Udaipur

After breakfast drive to Udaipur, en route visit Pushkar, and continue to Udaipur. On arrival check in at the hotel and take a rest. overnight stay at a hotel in Udaipur.


Day Day 07:- Udaipur

After breakfast enjoys the boat ride in Lake Pichhola and then visit city palace, saheliyon ki badi (friends garden) & Pratap memorial. Overnight stay at hotel.


Day 08 :- Udaipur to Srinagar

After breakfast drive to udaipur airport for flight o Srinagar for Himalayas. on arrival at the Srinagar Airport, Incredible Tour To India representative will assist and guide you to the resort or a houseboat. , you can take enjoy in the Shikara on Dal Lake. En route, you will see the Kabutarkhana, Bathing Boats, Floating Gardens, Char Chinar Island,, Floating Shops, and other popular tourist attractions. The best comparison of this picturesque scene is with Gondola rides in Venice. stay overnight.


Day 09:- Srinagar

After breakfast, we have an option for a city tour for half a day. visit places such as Nishat Bagh, famous Mughal Gardens, Shalimar Bagh. Afterward, after proceed to the Shankaracharya Temple at the top of a hill and return to the hotel in the evening and stay overnight


Day 10:- Srinagar - Gulmarg - Srinagar (102 km/ 3 hours- one way)

After breakfast at the hotel, you will be driven to Gulmarg, where we can explore the place by ourselves. we can take a cable car ride called "gondola ride" here or take pleasure in horse riding. In the evening, drive back to Srinagar and stay overnight

Day 11:- Srinagar - Sonamarg - Srinagar (159 km/ 5 hours- one way)

After at breakfast at Hotel, check out and drive to Sonamarg.. You explore pause at certain scenic spots and then continue the drive to Sonamarg, called the Meadow of Gold. You can visit the Thajiwas Glacier and take the pictures if you want. After enjoying the Sonamarg expedition, you drive back to Srinagar in the evening and return to the pre-booked hotel.

Day 12:- Srinagar - Pahalgam - Srinagar (181 km/ 5 hours- one way)

exploring the Kashmir tour, you enjoy the Pahalgam, after breakfast at the hotel. En route, short break and see the Avantipur ruin that once was a temple and the Saffron Fields. the Pahalgam is a spot where various Bollywood movies have been shoting. drive back to Srinagar in the evening and stay overnight.

Day 13:- Srinagar - Departure

after breakfast( complete the Rajasthan with Himalaya tour) drive to Srinagar airport for a flight to New Delhi and then fly back to home with sweet and incredible memories.
