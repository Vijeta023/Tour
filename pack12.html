overview



Tour of Rajasthan, Incredible Tours. Jaipur - Pushkar - Udaipur - Ranakpur Jain Temple - Jodhpur - Jaisalmer. This tour includes a visit to the fort and palaces of Jaipur, a fascinating city of exquisite architecture, forts, grand temples and monuments; Pushkar with its bathing ghats and golden temple is a picturesque destination; the opulent Bikaner Fort that lies around the citadel of Udaipur; Ranakpur is home to ancient Jain temples built by Lord Mahavira himself

Rajasthan Incredible Tour is a 8 days / 7 nights tour to Jaipur, Udaipur and Ranakpur Jain Temple. The tour also visits Pushkar Lake, Pushkar Camel Market and Sisodia Memorial Palace. The tour ends with a visit to Jodhpur which was founded by Maharaja Jai Singh II in 1527. This town is well known for its ancient palaces and wonderful climate!

Explore the incredible city of Jaipur in Rajasthan, before travelling to Pushkar, Udaipur and Ranakpur Jain temples. Discover Indian culture in this fascinating region by visiting an array of historical monuments, large and small. The journey continues with breathtaking views of royal palaces and adventure activities in the sun-blasted desert roads of Jodhpur and Jaisalmer.

Rajasthan Incredible Tours is a culturally rich tour with a focus on Indian history, architecture and famous forts. Explore some of the most beautiful monuments, ancient forts and gorgeous palaces with our tour that gives you an insight into Indian culture, history and heritage.

A tour where you will experience the culture, traditions and hospitality of the state of Rajasthan in India. Starting from Jaipur to Pushkar, Udaipur, Ranakpur Jain Temple, Jodhpur and Jaisalmer on a private luxury vehicle with the goal of bringing you closer to the people - real Indian experiences and memories that last for a lifetime.

Rajasthan Incredible Tours is a combination of 7 days / 6 nights group tour for the tourists visiting Jaipur, Pushkar and Ranakpur Jain Temple. It provides a safe travel experience with customized fittings to all the required tourist places.

This unique tour covers the highlights of Jaipur, Pushkar and Udaipur in Rajasthan and cruises across the legendary Lake Pichola and bewitching Lake Palace within Jodhpur. After visiting the Tajmahal, experience the mesmerizing beauty of Ranakpur Jain Temple and a night spent in the opulent palace that is built on top of a cliff in Udaipur.

This tour is for those who are looking for a relaxed, hassle-free and worthwhile holiday. The tours in Jaipur, Pushkar and Udaipur will take you to the awesome experience of the traditional culture, amazing architecture and heritage site of India. Your destination will be Ranakpur Jain Temple in Jodhpur which was built in 10th century BC. The temple has unique architecture which surpasses every part of India.






Itinerary



day 1 - Jaipur

Welcome to India, You will be received by our representative at international airport, we will be waiting for you, and by holding you play card in his hand. He will assist you to check in your hotel. In the Hotel you will have a brief description of your tour and the representative will hand over your documents regarding the tour. Overnight hotel in Jaipur for relaxing.


day 2 - Jaipur

After breakfast take a visit full day sightseeing include Amber Fort, water palace, Hawamahal (Palace of Winds), City Palace, Jantar-Mantar observatory, Overnight stay at the hotel. (Most invite at my home for dinner).


Jaipur – Pushkar – Udaipur

After breakfast drive to Udaipur, en route visit Pushkar. Continue to Udaipur. On arrival check in hotel and take rest. Evening join the Jugmandir Aarti. Stay overnight at hotel.


day 4 - Udaipur

After breakfast enjoy the boat ride in lake Pichhola and then visit city palace, Saheliyon ki Badi( friends garden) & Pratap memorial. Stay overnight at hotel.


Udaipur – Jodhpur

After breakfast drive to Jodhpur, en route visit Ranakpur jain temple. Continue to Jodhpur. After leisure rest visit Mehrangarh fort, Jaswant Tharra, Umaid Bhawan Palace and evening visit the clock tower market. Stay overnight at hotel.


day 6 - Jodhpur – Jaisalmer

After breakfast visit rest of monuments ( if we not cover in first day) and then proceed top Jaisalmer. On arrival check in hotel and evening visit the Jaisalmer fort( Golden fort). Stay overnight at hotel.


day 7 - Jaisalmer

After breakfast visit Patwon ki haveli, Nathmal Ki Haveli, Gaddisar Lake and walk in old city. After lunch drive to Sam( Thar and desert). Evening enjoy the camel ride and Jeep safari in desert. Dinner with folk dance and cultural program. Stay overnight at swis camp.


day 8 - Jaisalmer – Home

After breakfast ready for transfer to airport for flight to home with sweet and incredible memories. (Drive backs to Jodhpur for take the flight for Mumbai / Delhi).
