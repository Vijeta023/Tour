overview



The tour starts in New Delhi, where visitors can explore the city's rich cultural heritage, with a visit to the Qutub Minar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the Lotus Temple, a Bahai House of Worship.

Then, the tour goes to Agra, where visitors can see the Taj Mahal, one of the seven wonders of the world and the Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

The tour then heads to Gwalior, a city known for the Gwalior Fort and the Jai Vilas Palace.

Next, the tour goes to Khajuraho, a UNESCO World Heritage Site known for the Khajuraho Temples, a group of Hindu and Jain temples.

The tour then goes to Bandhavgarh and Kanha, two of the most popular national parks in India, known for tigers, leopards and other wild animals.
