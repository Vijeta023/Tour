overview



The Real Rajasthan tour itinerary is a comprehensive tour of the state of Rajasthan, which is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful palaces and forts, and the vibrant colors of its traditional attire and architecture. This tour covers some of the most popular tourist destinations in Rajasthan, including Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, Jaisalmer, Bikaner, and Pushkar. The tour typically includes visits to the famous forts and palaces, cultural shows, and traditional activities such as camel safari, boat rides, and cooking classes. The tour also includes accommodation in heritage hotels and resorts, which offer a unique experience of staying in a traditional Rajasthani palace or fort.






Itinerary



day 1 :-

Arrival in Jaipur and check-in at the hotel. Evening stroll through the bazaars of Jaipur and dinner at a traditional Rajasthani restaurant.


Day 2:

Visit to the Amber Fort and the City Palace in Jaipur. Afternoon visit to the Jantar Mantar observatory and the Hawa Mahal.


Day 3:

Drive to Pushkar and visit the famous Pushkar Lake and the Brahma Temple. Afternoon spent exploring the colorful markets of Pushkar.


Day 4:-

Drive to Udaipur and check-in at the hotel. Afternoon visit to the City Palace and the Jagdish Temple.


Day 5:

Boat ride on Lake Pichola and visit to the Jagmandir Island Palace. Afternoon visit to the Sahelion ki Bari gardens.


Day 6:

Drive to Jodhpur and check-in at the hotel. Afternoon visit to the Mehrangarh Fort and the Jaswant Thada memorial.


Day 7:

Visit to the Umaid Bhawan Palace and the Mandore Gardens in Jodhpur. Afternoon spent exploring the markets of Jodhpur.


Day 8:

Drive to Jaisalmer and check-in at the hotel. Evening visit to the Jaisalmer Fort and the Patwon ki Haveli.


Day 9:

Visit to the Sam Sand Dunes and an optional camel safari. Afternoon spent exploring the markets of Jaisalmer.


Day 10:

Drive back to Jaipur and check-in at the hotel. Evening farewell dinner and overnight stay in Jaipur.



Day 11:

Departure from Jaipur
