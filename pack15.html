overview



The 15 Days Rajasthan Tour is a comprehensive tour of the state of Rajasthan in India, which covers some of the most popular and iconic destinations in the region. The tour starts in the capital city of New Delhi and proceeds to the city of Agra, where the famous Taj Mahal is located. From Agra, the tour moves on to the pink city of Jaipur, famous for its forts and palaces.

Next, the tour heads to the holy city of Pushkar, known for its Pushkar Camel Fair and the only Brahma Temple in the world. The tour then continues to Udaipur, also known as the "Venice of the East" for its picturesque lakes and palaces.

The tour then moves on to Jodhpur, the "Blue City" famous for its Mehrangarh Fort, and the Jaswant Thada Memorial. From Jodhpur, the tour proceeds to Jaisalmer, the "Golden City" known for its rich history and architecture, including the Jaisalmer Fort, Havelis, and temples.

Further, the tour will be covering Bikaner, a desert city that is known for its forts and palaces. The final destination on the tour is Mandawa, a small town known for its beautiful havelis and fresco paintings.

Throughout the tour, you will have an opportunity to experience the vibrant culture of Rajasthan, including its food, music, dance, and art. Additionally, the tour will also give you a chance to interact with the local people, learn about their traditions and customs, and gain a deeper understanding of the history and culture of the region.
