overview



The Royal Rajasthan Tour itinerary is a comprehensive tour that takes you through some of the most iconic and picturesque destinations in Rajasthan, India. The tour starts in the bustling capital city of New Delhi, where you will have the opportunity to explore some of the city's famous monuments and historical sites, such as the Red Fort and the Qutub Minar.

From New Delhi, the tour moves on to Mandawa, a beautiful town known for its stunning havelis and frescoes. The next stop is Bikaner, a historic city famous for its forts, palaces, and temples. Here, you can visit the Junagarh Fort and the Laxmi Niwas Palace.

Royal Rajasthan Tour then takes you to Jaisalmer, a stunning desert city known for its medieval architecture and desert landscapes. Here, you can visit the Jaisalmer Fort and take a camel safari through the Thar Desert.

Next, Royal Rajasthan Tour moves on to Jodhpur, another historic city known for its impressive forts and palaces. Here, you can visit the Mehrangarh Fort and the Jaswant Thada Memorial.

From Jodhpur, the tour takes you to Ranakpur, a small town famous for its intricate Jain temples. You will have the opportunity to explore the temples and learn about the Jain religion and culture.

Royal Rajasthan Tour then takes you to Udaipur, a beautiful city known for its lakes, palaces, and temples. Here, you can visit the City Palace and take a boat ride on Lake Pichola.

Next, the tour takes you to Pushkar, a holy city famous for its temples and the Pushkar Camel Fair. Here, you can visit the Brahma Temple and witness the colorful fair.

Finally, Royal Rajasthan Tour ends in Jaipur, the "Pink City" known for its forts, palaces, and temples. Here, you can visit the Amber Fort, the Hawa Mahal, and the City Palace. The tour concludes with a visit to Agra, the city of the Taj Mahal.

The Royal Rajasthan tour offers a perfect mix of history, culture, and scenic beauty, providing an in-depth experience of the rich and diverse heritage of Rajasthan.






Itinerary



Day 1: Arrival in New Delhi

Meet and greet by Incredible Tour To India representative vat the airport and transfer to the hotel. Overnight stay in New Delhi.


Day 2: New Delhi

Sightseeing tour of New Delhi, visiting places like the Red Fort, Jama Masjid, Chandni Chowk, Raj Ghat, India Gate, and more. Overnight stay in New Delhi.


Day 3: New Delhi - Mandawa

Start the day early and drive to Mandawa, a small town famous for its frescoes and havelis. Visit the famous frescoes of the town and overnight stay in Mandawa.


Day 4: Mandawa - Bikaner

Drive to Bikaner and visit the famous Junagarh Fort, Laxmi Niwas Palace, and other notable places in the city. Overnight stay in Bikaner.


Day 5: Bikaner - Jaisalmer

Drive to Jaisalmer and visit the famous Jaisalmer Fort, Patwon Ki Haveli, and other notable places in the city. Overnight stay in Jaisalmer.


Day 6: Jaisalmer - Jodhpur

Drive to Jodhpur and visit the famous Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada, and other notable places in the city. Overnight stay in Jodhpur.


Day 7: Jodhpur - Ranakpur

Drive to Ranakpur and visit the famous Jain Temples. Overnight stay in Ranakpur.


Day 8: Ranakpur - Udaipur

Drive to Udaipur and visit the famous City Palace, Lake Pichola, and other notable places in the city. Overnight stay in Udaipur.


Day 9: Udaipur - Pushkar

Drive to Pushkar and visit the famous Brahma Temple, Pushkar Lake, and other notable places in the city. Overnight stay in Pushkar.


Day 10: Pushkar - Jaipur

Drive to Jaipur and visit the famous Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and other notable places in the city. Overnight stay in Jaipur.



Day 11: Jaipur - Agra

Drive to Agra and visit the famous Taj Mahal, Agra Fort, and other notable places in the city. Overnight stay in Agra.



Day 12: Agra - New Delhi

Drive back to New Delhi and transfer to the airport for your onward journey.
