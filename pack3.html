overview



The Incredible Rajasthan Tour places you in the heart of Rajasthan’s ancient ruins, temples and palaces. This beautiful tour will venture through exotic Indian landscapes including Jaipur, Udaipur and Jodhpur. Visiting old forts and palaces is one thing; experiencing them from a different perspective takes it to a new realm! Traveling with our experienced crew, you will get to experience the royal lifestyle of North India as they show you around their magnificent homes, temples and sights.

Join us on the Incredible Rajasthan with Varanasi tour, a perfect blend of cultural, historical and contemporary experiences. You will be visiting Agra and Jaipur before reaching to your next destination in Udaipur. Here you will be visiting Haveli Palace, Fatehpur Sikri and will also get a glimpse of Ranthambore tiger reserve. Evening after evening, you will enjoy the mesmerizing sunset across the Indian desert as you head for Jodhpur.

Explore the spectacular Golden Triangle of India with The Golden Triangle is a popular tourist destination, so expect to see some other travelers. You’ll also have plenty of opportunities to meet people from all over the world during this trip. Throughout this trip, you’ll be accompanied by an English-speaking guide and driver. You’ll stay in three- to four-star hotels and lodges that are comfortable and convenient for sightseeing.

Discover the majestic palaces and heritage of Jaipur, Fatehpur Sikri and Udaipur. Stay close to nature in the desert of Pushkar, then splurge on a royal tour of the city of wealth, gold and jewels - Jaipur. Travel by train through charming villages and desert landscapes to reach stunning Pushkar. See the many ghats along our river Ganga, some for cremation, others for bathing.

Itinerary



Day 01: New Delhi (Arrival)

Welcome to India, You will be received by Incredible Tour To India representative at international airport, we will be waiting for you, and by holding you play card in his hand.

He will assist you to check in your hotel. In the Hotel you will have a brief description of your tour and the representative will hand over your documents regarding the tour. Overnight hotel in Delhi for relaxing.


Day 02: New Delhi & Old Delhi Expedition

After breakfast Driver pick up – City Tour visit Jama Masjid, Humayun Tomb, Qutub Minar, Parliament House, Rajghat, India Gate, Lotus Temple, evening enjoy local market. Rickshaw Ride (in the local market) Overnight stay at New Delhi.


Day 03: New Delhi to Varanasi (By flight)

After breakfast drive to airport for fly to Varanasi. On arrival assist by driver and direct to hotel. After leisure rest join Ghats Aarti. Overnight at hotel.


Day 04: Varanasi

Varanasi Early morning boat excursion on the river Ganges, a ferry pilgrimage from ghat to ghat. People bathe early in the morning to offer the prayers to the rising sun. Return back to hotel for breakfast and enjoy excursion tour to Sarnath. Sarnath, 10 km from Varanasi, where the Buddha preached his first sermon in the Deer Park, contains the most impressive remains, as well as a modem temple. Overnight at Hotel. .


Day 05: Varanasi to Khajuraho (By flight)

After breakfast drive to the airport for your flight to Khajuraho. On arrival you will be transferred to your hotel. Khajuraho is renowned for its erotic sculptures that adorn the many medieval Hindu and Jain temples.

There are many interpretations of the risqué carvings, and your guide will provide you with more insight into these architectural marvels. In the afternoon, visit the Chandela Temples. Overnight Khajuraho.


Day 06: Khajuraho to Orchha

170 KM, 03:00 hours Journey. After breakfast drive to Orchha. On arrival assist by driver and take leisure rest. After fresh n up visit Amar Mahal, Chaturbhuj Mandir, Ram Raja Mandir and Laxminarayan Temple . Overnight at hotel.


Day 07: Orchha to Agra

230 KM, 04:00 Hours journey. After breakfast drive to Agra. On arrival check in hotel. After fresh n up visit the Red Fort (Agra fort) and sunset Tajmahal (the sign of love). Overnight at hotel.


Day 08: Agra – Jaipur

240 KM, 04:00 hours journey. After breakfast visit sunrise Tajmahal and then drive to Jaipur. En route visit Fatehpur sikri. Continue to Jaipur. On arrival check in hotel and take rest. Evening walk around hotel. Overnight at hotel.


Day 09: Jaipur

After breakfast take a visit full day sightseeing include Amber Fort. Here at Amber, you’ll ascend till the gates of majestic Amber fort on elephant back. The fort displays a beautiful mix of Mughal and Rajput styles of architecture. Hawamahal (Palace of Winds).

The beauty of Jaipur is that its unique architecture and town planning is visible at even the most common places such as its squares and lanes. City Palace, Jantar-Mantar observatory, Overnight stay at the hotel. .


Day 10 & 11: Jaipur to Lakshman Sagar

230 KM, 03:30 hours journey. After breakfast drive to Lakshman Sagar resort for relaxing.



Day 12: Lakshman sagar to Udaipur

205 KM, 03:10 hours journey. After breakfast drive to Udaipur, on arrival check in hotel and take rest. Overnight at hotel. 230 KM, 03:30 hours journey. After breakfast drive to Lakshman Sagar resort for relaxing.



Day 13: Udaipur

After breakfast enjoy the Boat ride in Lake Pichhola. Visit the City Palace, Sahelion-ki-bari gardens, the Jagdish temple and the local Museum Overnight in Udaipur .



Day 14: Udaipur – Rohet Garh

After breakfast drive to Rohet garh, en route visit Ranakpur. On arrival check in hotel anRohet garhd experience the ancient, modern and Royal Rajasthan. Overnight at hotel.



Day 15: Rohet Garh to Jaisalmer

310 KM, 05:20 hours journey. After breakfast drive to Jaisalmer (Golden City). On arrival check in the desert camp and evening enjoy the camel ride and jeep safari. Overnight in desert camp.( dinner with folk dance and cultural program).



Day 16: Jaisalmer

After breakfast, visit the magnificent fort, the Patwon Ki Haveli ( Mansion) and Salim Singh Ki Haveli with their carved balconies and brackets which are supreme examples of the art. Overnight stay at hotel.



Day 17: Jaisalmer to Jodhpur

290 KM, 04:15 hours journey. After breakfast drive to Jodhpur. On arrival check in Hotel and relax. After rest visit Mehrangarh fort, Jaswant Thada & Clock Tower market evening visit Mandore Garden for leisure. Overnight at hotel.



Day 18: Jodhpur to New Delhi

After breakfast drive to airport for flight to New Delhi. On arrival assist by driver and overnight at hotel.



Day 19: New Delhi

After breakfast (timely) transfer to airport for flight back to home with sweet and incredible memories
