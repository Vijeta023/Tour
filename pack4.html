overview



The Rajasthan tour is the smallest and most expansive of the three great circles that constitute the Indo-Gangetic plain. It is bounded by Delhi, Agra and Jaipur on either side and connected by road to Jodhpur. The region contains some of India's most important temples and other cultural landmarks, including iconic monuments in Delhi such as Red Fort, India Gate and Humayun's Tomb; Agra's Taj Mahal Palace; Jaipur's Hawa Mahal; Jaisalmer Fort; Udaipur's Fatehsagar Lake; Pushkar Lake and Mathura Temple.

The Golden Triangle is also home to the cities of Agra and Jaipur, both major tourist destinations in their own right. It is surrounded by the Aravalli Range in the west and Vindhya Range in the east. Temple. The Rajasthan tour also contains many other sites of interest including the Amber Fort in Jaipur, built in 1592; and the Mehrangarh Fort in Jodhpur, which was built in 1459. Temple. The triangle is also home to two World Heritage Sites:

the Nanda Devi National Park and Rajasthan's Desert National Park. Temple. The Golden Triangle is also known as the "Platonic Ideal" of Indian tourism, and was named so because it is considered by many to be the most complete representation of all that India has to offer.

Rajasthan is famous for its culture and heritage. Itinerary



Day 01: Arrive New Delhi

Welcome to India, You will be received by Incredible Tour To India representative at international airport, we will be waiting for you, and by holding you play card in his hand. He will assist you to check in your hotel. In the Hotel you will have a brief description of your tour and the representative will hand over your documents regarding the tour. Overnight hotel in Delhi for relaxing.


Day 02: Old Delhi

After breakfast visit on a sightseeing tour Old Delhi in the morning covering Jama Mosque, and Mahatma Gandhis memorial Raj Ghat. In the afternoon, set off on a sightseeing tour of New Delhi covering Safdarjang’s Tomb, Qutab Minar and Humayun’s Tomb. Overnight at hotel.


Day 03: Delhi to Agra

240 KM, 4 hours journey. After breakfast drive to Agra, On arrival check in hotel. Later day visit the Taj Mahal, Agra fort, I’timad-ud-Daulah tomb. Evening at leisure. Overnight at hotel.


Day 04: Agra to Jaipur

250 KM, 04:30 hours journey. After breakfast drive to Jaipur, en route visit fatehpur sikri, on arrival check in Hotel and take rest. Evening visit Jal Mahal and local market. Overnight at hotel. (Mostly invite at my home for dinner).


Day 05: Jaipur

After breakfast take a visit full day sightseeing include Amber Fort. Here at Amber, you’ll ascend till the gates of majestic Amber fort on elephant back .The fort displays a beautiful mix of Mughal and Rajput styles of architecture. Hawamahal (Palace of Winds).

The beauty of Jaipur is that its unique architecture and town planning is visible at even the most common places such as its squares and lanes. City Palace, Janter Manta observatory, Overnight stay at the hotel.


Day 06: Jaipur to Village

After breakfast drive to village for experience the real and rural life of India. on arrival welcome by family and take the leisure rest. after rest walk in village and know about the real India. stay overnight at home.


Day 07: Village to Bikaner

330 KM, 04:45 hours journey. After breakfast drive to Bikaner, on arrival check in Hotel, after leisure rest visit the Junagarh fort, Lalgarh palace and evening visit the camel breeding centre. Overnight at hotel.


Day 08: Bikaner

After breakfast enjoy the experience of royalty of luxurious rural Rajasthan and visit in Village by Jeep and join the Lakshman sagar activity. Overnight at hotel.


Day 09: Bikaner to Jaisalmer

330 KM, 04:45 hours journey. After breakfast drive to Jaisalmer. On arrival check in hotel and take rest, after fresh n up visit Badda wagh & Gaddisar Lake. Overnight at hotel.


Day 10: Jaisalmer

After breakfast visit the magnificent fort, the Patwon Ki Haveli ( Mansion) and Salim singh Ki Haveli, with their carved balconies and brackets which are supreme examples of the art. Afternoon drive to desert and evening enjoy the camel ride and jeep safari in desert. Late evening back to Hotel. Overnight at hotel.



Day 11: Jaisalmer to Jodhpur

After breakfast drive to Jodhpur. On arrival check in Hotel and relax. Overnight at hotel.



Day 12: Jodhpur

After breakfast visit Mehrangarh fort, Jaswant tharra & clock tower market evening visit Mandore Garden for leisure. Overnight at hotel.



Day 13: Jodhpur to Rawla Narlai

After breakfast drive to Rawla Narlai. On arrival tradinational welcome by Rawla Narlai. After leisure rest walk in village and know about the Indian life and evening expriemnce the royalty of Rajasthan. Overnight at hotel.



Day 14: Rawla Narlai to Udaipur

After breakfast drive to Udaipur, en route visit Ranakpur jain Temple. Continue to Udaipur. On arrival check in hotel and take rest. Overnight at hotel.



Day 15: Udaipur

After breakfast enjoy the Boat ride in Lake Pichhola. Visit the City Palace, Sahelion-ki-bari gardens & lok kalla Mandir. Overnight at hotel.



Day 16: Udaipur to Varanasi

After breakfast drive to airport for fly to Varanasi. On arrival assist by driver and direct to hotel. After leisure rest join Ghats Aarti. Overnight at hotel.



Day 17 & 18: Varanasi (Guide)

Early morning boat excursion on the river Ganges, a ferry pilgrimage from ghat to ghat. People bathe early in the morning to offer the prayers to the rising sun.

Return back to hotel for breakfast and enjoy excursion tour to Sarnath. Sarnath, 10 km from Varanasi, where the Buddha preached his first sermon in the Deer Park, contains the most impressive remains, as well as a modem temple. Overnight at Hotel.



Day 19: Varanasi to New Delhi

After breakfast drive to airport for flight to New Delhi and then flight back to home with sweet and incredible memories.
