overview



This tour is designed for those who want to combine culture, adventure and history in Rajasthan. The route crosses many ancient monuments including Jaipur and Bikaner, known as the Pink City. From there you’ll travel to Jaisalmer, a desert oasis adored by Poors and tourists alike.

After travelling through camel routes you will make your way to Jodhpur and then Udaipur. This city is famous for its Lake Palace and the largest lake in the world, but it’s also home to some of the oldest temples in India.

Following this stop in Rajasthan you’re heading east to reached Haridwar & Rishikesh before heading south to Mount Kailash & Manali before moving on to Dharamsala and Delhi via Shimla

This is part of the World Heritage Site of Machu Picchu and Patagonia that we will visit en route to Laddakh and Ladakh.

Delight yourself with the beauty of Rajasthan. Starting from the magnificent capital New Delhi, take a tour through the popular tourist destinations of Jaipur, Udaipur, Jaisalmer and Jodhpur.

Discover New Delhi's monuments such as Jama Masjid, Chandni Chowk, Humayun's Tomb and The Parliament House. Cross over to Bikaner where see beautiful forts and palaces overlooking the desert.

You'll then head to Jaisalmer in Kutch district of western Rajasthan. Explore shikaras and jewel-colored forts dating back to era of camel caravans. Next visit Jaisalmer Desert Safari Park and a camel ride followed by dinner at Le Palmier restaurant.

Next day travel further south-eastward to Bikaner which has many interesting places like Sabatgarh Fort, Doongars Museum and Balaji Temple Complex which was built by Akbar in 1610!

Enjoy one night stay at Churu city or move ahead towards Jodhpur city where see spectacular Umaid Bhavan fort before reaching Jodhpur city which is known for its museums like Lal Mahal palace in former Mughal times, Fatehsagar Lake etc.
